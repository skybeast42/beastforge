# tracks/

The beast's **trail** — everything it produces that isn't bounded reproducible
work (`work/`) or an app (`apps/`): notes, ideas, standing areas (system setup,
etc.), brainstorms / explorations, and the beast's own captures. Free-form,
unbounded — **not brief-gated, no closure**. (You *may* still contract a bounded
slice of track work from a `work/` brief when a plan helps; tracks just never
*require* one — the brief lives in `work/`, the trail here.)

Provenance is marked by the **`00_` prefix**:

- **`00_`-prefixed = beast-laid / reserved** — `00_insights/` (the capture
  channel, consumed by `/deck`; holds the `/tokens` report too) and beast-level
  deliverables (e.g. a `/deck` saved to `00_decks/`).
- **everything else = user-laid** — freely named (`system_setup/`, `ideas/`,
  `recipes/`, …).

`audits/` lives here too — `/audit` writes `tracks/audits/<scope>/` (assessment is
the beast's trail *about* its own work).

A track is its own record; no schema. Both footprints share the trail — the
beast's are marked, yours are free.
