A slide-deck template.

SkyBeast theme — five layouts + a code-block primitive, self-contained, offline, embeddable. Replace these slides with your own.

Default · statement

A centred column.

No layout class needed — best for a statement or a short list. It sits centred, so it reads balanced at any width. The kit:

  • slide--title — the opener
  • slide — a centred statement (this one)
  • slide--split — text beside a graphic
  • slide--full — a wide band for a big visual

slide--split

Text one side, a graphic the other.

Two full-height 50/50 columns — put your point on the left and an image, inline SVG, or chart on the right. On mobile (and in the embedded preview) they stack.

your image · SVG · chart here

slide--full

A wide band — for a big visual, or one large line.

Wider than the reading column. Drop in a diagram or a full-width image (<img style="width:100%">), or just let a single bold statement breathe.

slide--photo

Full-bleed image, caption overlay.

A real <img> fills the slide; this overlay sits bottom-left with a red accent line and blurred glass. Omit .caption for a silent visual.

primitive · <pre>

A code block, for configs or snippets.

Subtle surface, hairline border, monospace — fits the dark void without shouting. Scrolls horizontally on overflow.
